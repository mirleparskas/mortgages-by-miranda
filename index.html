Quick answers

Direct answers for search engines, AI tools, and real borrowers.

What does Mortgages by Miranda do?

Mortgages by Miranda provides mortgage broker support in Ontario for home purchases, renewals, refinancing, pre-approvals, self-employed borrowers, and investment property financing.

Who should contact a mortgage broker before shopping?

First-time buyers, borrowers with variable income, people carrying debt, homeowners near renewal, and anyone planning to refinance should speak with a broker before making a major decision.

What documents are usually needed?

Common documents include government ID, pay stubs, job letters, tax documents, bank statements showing down payment, mortgage statements, property tax bills, and details for debts or existing properties.

Is the lowest mortgage rate always best?

No. A mortgage should be compared by rate, penalties, prepayment privileges, portability, restrictions, lender service, approval conditions, and how long the borrower expects to keep the mortgage.

Mortgage glossary

Terms borrowers search for before they call.

Stress test
A qualification check that tests whether a borrower can afford payments at a higher benchmark rate than the contract mortgage rate.
Insured mortgage
A mortgage with default insurance, usually required when a buyer has less than 20 percent down on an eligible purchase.
Prepayment privilege
The amount a borrower can pay toward the mortgage early without a penalty, often shown as a percentage of the original balance each year.
Debt service ratio
A lender calculation comparing housing costs and other debts to qualifying income.
